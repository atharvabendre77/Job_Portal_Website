Marketing Manager

Pune/Kolhapur, Maharashtra Full Time/Part Time 15000-25000

Job description

The Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and executing comprehensive marketing strategies. They oversee advertising, market research, and budget management to enhance brand awareness and drive business growth. Collaboration with cross-functional teams ensures cohesive branding and effective communication.

Responsibility

Develop and implement strategic marketing plans to achieve company objectives. Manage marketing campaigns across various channels, including digital, print, and social media.

  • Develop and implement strategic marketing plans to achieve company objectives.
  • Analyze market trends and competitor activity to identify opportunities for growth.
  • Manage and allocate budget effectively to maximize ROI and achieve marketing goals.
  • Oversee the creation and execution of marketing campaigns across various channels (digital, print, events, etc.).
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment of marketing efforts with overall business objectives.

Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, Communications, or a related field; MBA or advanced degree preferred.

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, or a related field; MBA preferred.
  • Strong analytical skills, with the ability to interpret data and make strategic decisions.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with internal teams and external partners.
  • Proven track record of successful marketing campaigns and initiatives
  • With at least 5 years of experience in a marketing management role.
